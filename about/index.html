Company Profile

OUR FOUNDATION

Vision & Mission

To be the most trusted manufacturer of RCC Spun Pipes in India by delivering products that meet and exceed Indian Standard specifications. We are committed to innovation in concrete manufacturing, sustainable practices, and building long-term relationships with our clients through reliability and quality.

  • 100% compliance with IS 458 specifications
  • In-house quality control laboratory
  • Automated centrifugal spinning process
  • Timely supply and project coordination

Manufacturing Capability

Our manufacturing plant is equipped with modern centrifugal spinning machines capable of producing pipes ranging from 200mm to 1800mm internal diameter. The plant operates in three shifts to meet the high-volume demands of large infrastructure projects without compromising on quality.

  • Production capacity: 500+ pipes per day
  • Pipe diameter range: 200mm - 1800mm
  • NP2, NP3, NP4 class manufacturing
  • Spigot & Joint and T&G configurations

Infrastructure & Material Handling

Our manufacturing facility is equipped with modern infrastructure and advanced material handling systems to ensure efficient production, safe operations, and timely deliveries. From automated raw material handling to heavy-duty lifting equipment, every stage of the production process is designed to maximize productivity while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

  • 4 spinning machinery units for manufacturing pipes of various diameters
  • Semi-automated conveyor belt system for accurate raw material handling and higher diameter pipe production
  • EOT crane system with heavy-duty remote-controlled hoists for safe production, loading, and unloading operations
  • 4 dedicated curing tanks for proper curing and strength development of pipes
  • 8-acre stockyard for efficient storage and dispatch management
  • Adequate fleet of transport vehicles ensuring timely deliveries
  • Sufficient mould inventory to fulfill large-volume project requirements within committed timelines
